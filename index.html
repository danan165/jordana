About

Jordan Jinosko (she/hers) and Dana Nehls (she/hers) first met in a music theory class at the University of Michigan in the fall of 2016. They started out performing for friends, and then at local open mics in Ann Arbor. Since then, they have enjoyed performing together in the Greater Detroit Area, playing a mixture of jazz standards, indie love songs, and vintage ballads from the 1950s.

Jordan is a songwriter and orchestral composer, with music on Spotify and various streaming platforms. She is based in Ann Arbor, MI.

Instruments: Vocals, Acoustic Guitar, Electric Guitar, Piano
